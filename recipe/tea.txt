## My TEA RECIPE FOR THREE PEOPLE:
[] Boil three cups of water for 10 minutes.
[] Pour the boiled water into three cups.
[] Add a tea bag into each cup filled with hot water.
[] Stir the hot water in the cup using a tea spoon.
[] Remove the tea bag out of the cup.
[] Add three tea spoons of sugar into each cup.
[] Stir until the sugar dissolves.
[] Tea is ready to be served.
